## Thinking in Boundaries

I keep returning to systems thinking because it forces me to look at interfaces, failure modes, and ownership before I reach for implementation detail. It is the fastest way I know to avoid accidental complexity.

## Why It Matters in Small Projects

Even a small personal project benefits from explicit boundaries. The moment content, routing, and UI concerns start blending together, iteration slows down and quality becomes harder to preserve.
